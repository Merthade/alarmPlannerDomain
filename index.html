How it works

From "I must not forget that" to done, in three steps.

1

Pick a date

Tap the add button and choose any day on the calendar: tomorrow, next month, or half a year out.

2

Set the details

Choose the time, a repeat pattern if you need one, a sound, and a color-coded tag like Work or Travel.

3

Forget about it

The alarm waits quietly until its day, then rings even on Silent. One-off alarms archive themselves after firing.

Frequently asked questions

Can I set an iPhone alarm for a specific date in the future?

The built-in Clock app cannot attach a date to an alarm, only days of the week. Alarm Clock Planner lets you pick any date on a calendar, weeks or months ahead, and the alarm fires once on that day and archives itself. Learn more

Can an iPhone alarm repeat monthly or yearly?

Not in the built-in Clock app, which only repeats on days of the week. Alarm Clock Planner supports monthly, yearly, every other week, and other custom intervals, with an optional end date. Learn more

Will my alarm still ring on Silent mode or in a Focus?

Yes. Alarm Clock Planner is built on Apple's AlarmKit framework, so alarms ring like system alarms: through the Silent switch, Do Not Disturb, and Focus modes. Learn more

Can I skip one occurrence of a repeating alarm?

Yes. Skip next occurrence silences just the next ring and keeps the schedule armed, so you cannot forget to switch the alarm back on. Learn more

Can I change how long snooze lasts?

Yes, snooze is adjustable from 1 to 30 minutes per alarm, and you can cap how many times an alarm can be snoozed before it stops for real. Learn more

Can I use my own sound as the alarm?

Yes. Import any audio file from the Files app, and it is trimmed to the 30 second alarm limit automatically. 19 built-in sounds are included, and sounds can be set per alarm, per group, or as a default. Learn more

How do I keep a lot of alarms organized on iPhone?

Color-coded tags group your alarms. Toggle a whole group at once, bulk-edit several alarms, sort by date, time, or group, and give each group its own sound. Learn more

Can I set a quick nap alarm?

Yes. Press and hold the add button for one-tap nap presets: 10, 15, 20, or 30 minutes, or nap until a set time. Naps are unlimited. Learn more

Can I set an alarm for a flight that is weeks away?

Yes. Pick the flight date on the calendar the moment you book, set the wake-up time, and tag it Travel. The alarm sits quietly until that morning and rings even on Silent. Learn more

Can I see my next alarm on the iPhone Lock Screen?

Yes. Lock Screen and Home Screen widgets show your next alarm with a countdown, and the Home Screen widget can toggle an alarm without opening the app. Learn more

Are my alarms backed up if I get a new phone?

Yes. With iCloud Backup on (the default when you are signed in to iCloud), your alarms, groups, custom sounds, and settings are saved to your own private iCloud, and the app offers to restore them on a new device or after reinstalling. It is a restore point, not live two-way sync. Learn more

Do I need an account to use Alarm Clock Planner?

No. There is no account or sign-up. Download Alarm Clock Planner from the App Store and start setting alarms right away. Get the app
